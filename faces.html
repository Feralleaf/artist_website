A collection of portraits and quick sketches drawn by the artist. His inspiration and ideas are influenced by strangers and relatives. The time taken to produce each one varies from 20 mins for a quick sketch to hours for a detailed portrait. The majority of the artist's work are produced digitally with a few exceptions such as 'Dreamy' being sketched in pencil.
